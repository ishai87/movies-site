How we started..

Our partnership began during our studies at the Emek Yezreel College.
As part of a project in an Internet technology course, we chose to build a website for films.
This site was built with great investment and thorough planning in order to give you comfort and enjoyment.

Vision

Our vision is to provide you with the latest and best movies.
The site is divided into two sections, for children under the age of 13 and for adults over the age of 13.
On the site you will find everything you want to know about the films, from their category and the actors to the rating.
